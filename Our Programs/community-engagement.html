Community Engagement Program

Key Features of the Program

LNA

Local Need Assessment

Our projects begin with an assessment to identify the community's needs and challenges. Participants engage with residents, organizations, and stakeholders to gather insights and prioritize areas for action.

SBW

SKill-Building Workshops

Participants have the opportunity to share their unique skills and knowledge through workshops that benefit community members. Topics may include digital literacy, financial management, health and wellness, and environmental sustainability.

CHE

Cultural and Heritage Events

We celebrate the diverse cultural backgrounds within our communities by organizaing events that highlight ocal traditions, arts, and heritage, fostering community pride and engagement

ECI

Environmental Cleanu-up Initiatives

Our youth-led clean-up initiatives aim to enhance the environmental quality of local parks, streets, and public spaces. Participants work together to promote environmental stewardship and awareness.

YLAC

Youth-Led Advocay Campaigns

Participants lead advocacy campaigns addressing social, environmental, or political issues affecting their communities. Through these campaigns, they develop skills in grassroots organizing, public speaking, and effective communication strategies.
