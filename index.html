REFRAME

Reframe is a platform designed to connect and amplify the work of refugee leaders across the world. From digital livelihoods and climate change, to education and emergencies like the war in Sudan, refugee organisations are on the frontline of the world’s biggest and most urgent crises. Whether you're a storyteller, activist, organisation or donor, Reframe is a hub for refugees leading positive change and inspiring action in their communities.
Visit reframe

SUPPORT SUDAN

Donate to Refugee-Led Organisations on the frontlines, responding to the urgent needs of displaced communities affected by the war. Your support helps deliver critical aid, shelter, healthcare, and hope. Together, we can rebuild lives. Get involved today!

UNITY

Unity is at the heart of what we do at Cohere. By coming together, we can create a world where every voice is heard, and refugee communities across the African continent have the resources they need to transform their futures. Get involved today!

ARE WE ALL LISTENING?

With displacement rising, effective responses start by listening—to the expertise, experiences, and ideas of refugee leaders shaping solutions for their communities. Listening deepens our understanding, expands knowledge, and strengthens humanity. Together, we can amplify their voices and create lasting change.
